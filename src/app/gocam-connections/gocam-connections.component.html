{{siteName}} GO-CAM pathways Documentation

GO-CAMs, or Gene Ontology Causal Activity Models, are a framework developed within the Gene Ontology (GO) project to represent complex biological pathways in a structured, computable format.
For more information, visit the PomBase GO-CAM documentation or the Gene Ontology project's GO-CAM overview.

We maintain a list of activities known or suspected to occur in fission yeast but not yet associated with a gene product. These “pathway holes” have been identified through GO-CAM causal model curation.
