### Over 50 *S. pombe* microPublications
<!-- pombase_flags: frontpage -->
<!-- newsfeed_thumbnail: pombase-logo-32x32px.png -->

The *S. pombe* research community has now published over 50 articles
at [microPublication Biology](https://www.micropublication.org/journals/biology)
(currently 53).

You can find the complete list on the
[*S. pombe* micropublications page](https://www.micropublication.org/journals/biology/species/s-pombe).

[microPublication Biology](https://www.micropublication.org) publishes
brief scientific reports based on data presented in a single
multi-panel figure. Article contents include novel findings, new
methods and reagents, negative results, replication of previously
published experiments. Micropublications are peer-reviewed and
PubMed-indexed, and are a perfect way to publish incidental findings
and small projects such as undergraduate research projects that would
not fit a traditional publication.  Thanks to all the authors who
published micropublications, the microPublication Biology team and the
Scientific Editors Charlie Hoffman, Sarah Sabatinos and Sarah Lambert!
