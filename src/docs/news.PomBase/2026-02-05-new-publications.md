### New PomBase collaborative publications
<!-- pombase_flags: frontpage -->
<!-- newsfeed_thumbnail: pombase-logo-32x32px.png -->

We recently contributed to 3 collaborative publications:

 - **Mondo: Integrating Disease Terminology Across Communities** \
   [N.A. Vasilevsky *et al.* *Genetics* October 2025, iyaf215](https://doi.org/10.1093/genetics/iyaf215) \
   Describes the Mondo disease ontology and applications
 - **GOFlowLLM - Curating miRNA literature with Large Language Models and flowcharts** \
   [A.F. Green *et al.* *Bioinformatics* Jan 2026, btaf683](https://doi.org/10.1093/bioinformatics/btaf683) \
   Supporting the evaluation of AI-extracted annotation from publications
 - **RNAcentral in 2026: Genes and literature integration** \
   [A.F. Green *et al.* *Nucleic Acids Res*. Dec 2025, gkaf1329](https://doi.org/10.1093/nar/gkaf1329) \
   The RNACentral database update

Please update your citation details for GO and RNACentral to the new publications.
