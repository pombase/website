### Transmembrane domain predictions from DeepTMHMM added
<!-- pombase_flags: frontpage -->
<!-- newsfeed_thumbnail: pombase-logo-32x32px.png -->

PomBase now includes transmembrane helix and signal peptide
predictions from [DeepTMHMM](https://dtu.biolib.com/DeepTMHMM/),
alongside the existing
[TMHMM](https://services.healthtech.dtu.dk/services/TMHMM-2.0/),
signal peptide predictions and experimental resources. 

A list of genes with predicted signal peptides and a list of those
with predicted TM domains is available in the "Commonly used queries"
section of the [Advanced Search](/query).  In the "Genes with specific features"
tab look for "Genes with predicted signal peptide" and "Genes with TM
domain predicted by DeepTMHMM".  Direct links to those lists:

  - [genes with predicted signal peptide](https://www.pombase.org/results/from/id/56df0d38-f5ce-47de-a22f-40f58059a1f3)
  - [genes with TM domains predicted by DeepTMHMM](https://www.pombase.org/results/from/id/0d464331-8f12-4f38-aa92-cdcb93c4f2db)

You can also query DeepTMHMM and TMHMM separately in the "Domains,
features and motifs" section of the [Advanced Search interface](/query)
if you require proteins with a specific number of TM domains.
