### Exploring the fission yeast Unknown Proteome:
<!-- newsfeed_thumbnail: pombase-logo-32x32px.png -->

Despite decades of research, many eukaryotic genes still lack clearly
defined functions. The [Unknown Protein Dashboard](/status/protein-status-tracker)
from PomBase helps researchers explore this "unknome" in the *S. pombe*.
The protein status resource now provides additional gene lists and new
or updated features.

The resource also highlights "missing activities"—biological functions
expected to occur in fission yeast but not yet linked to a gene
identified through pathway modelling with Gene Ontology Causal
Activity Models (GO-CAM).
