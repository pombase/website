### 13th International Fission Yeasts Meeting, July 12-16, 2027

**Save the date!**

*From the organisers:*

The **13th International Fission Yeasts Meeting** will take place from
**July 12 to 16, 2027** in **Bordeaux, France**. The meeting will be held in
the beautiful and historic [Palais de la Bourse](https://www.bordeauxpalaisbourse.com/en),
located in the heart of the city and on the quays of the Garonne
River.

We will be updating with more information as well as a meeting website
in the near future. Looking forward to seeing everyone at the meeting!
