#### Notes

Consensus sequences use the [IUBMB Nomenclature for Incompletely Specified Bases in Nucleic Acid Sequences](https://www.qmul.ac.uk/sbcs/iubmb/misc/naseq.html); briefly:

 -   R = purine (A or G)
 -   Y = pyrimidine (C or T)
 -   M = A or C
 -   W = A or T
 -   S = G or C
 -   K = G or T
 -   V = A, C or G
 -   H = A, C or T
 -   D = A, G or T
 -   B = C, G or T
